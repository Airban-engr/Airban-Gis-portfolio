Our Survey Process

  1. Site Visit & GNSS Data Collection – We visit your land with GNSS equipment to collect accurate coordinates.
  2. Boundary Analysis & Mapping – We process the data, define parcel edges, and prepare a clean plan.
  3. Site Plan Production – We produce a professional site plan with coordinates, bearings, scale, and north arrow.
  4. Submission-Ready Output – Plans are prepared for Lands Commission or district assembly requirements.
