Topographic Surveying

Topographic Survey in Ghana for Design, Drainage & Construction

Airban Engineering provides accurate topographic surveys, elevation data, contours, spot levels and feature mapping for architects, engineers, developers, contractors and landowners.

A topographic survey gives a detailed picture of the land surface. It shows ground levels, slopes, contours, existing structures, drains, roads, trees and other important site features. This information is essential before design, planning or construction begins.

What is a topographic survey?

A topographic survey maps the shape and features of a site. It helps designers understand how the land rises, falls and drains. Without this information, building and infrastructure designs may be based on assumptions instead of real ground conditions.

What we capture

  • Ground spot levels and contour lines
  • Existing buildings and structures
  • Roads, paths, walls and fences
  • Drains, culverts and watercourses
  • Visible utility features such as poles and manholes
  • Trees, landscape features and site constraints

Why topographic surveys matter

Accurate terrain data supports better engineering decisions. It helps with drainage design, earthworks planning, platform design, road alignment, flood risk checks and general site development.

Who needs this service?

  • Architects preparing site layouts and building designs
  • Engineers designing drainage, roads and infrastructure
  • Developers assessing site constraints before construction
  • Contractors planning earthworks and site preparation
  • Landowners who need to understand slopes and drainage risks

Our topographic survey process

  1. Project brief: We discuss your site, design needs and required deliverables.
  2. Field survey: We collect accurate levels and site features using surveying equipment.
  3. Data processing: We process the field data into contours, spot levels and mapped features.
  4. Final output: We provide clear survey plans and digital outputs for design or planning use.
